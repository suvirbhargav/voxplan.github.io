Voice-First iOS App

About VoxPlan

VoxPlan is an iOS calendar assistant that lets you create, view, and manage calendar events using your voice. Instead of tapping through multiple screens, simply speak your event details — VoxPlan handles the rest. It works with your existing calendars including iOS Calendar.

Powered by Mistral AI, Europe’s leading AI lab. Your data is processed securely and in compliance with GDPR.

Features

  • Create calendar events by speaking naturally — just say what, when, and where
  • Automatic extraction of event title, date, time, duration, and location from your voice input
  • View and manage your upcoming events in a clean, intuitive interface
  • Supports iOS Calendar
  • Privacy-first design — no tracking, no ads, no data selling
  • Audio is processed only when you explicitly tap the microphone

How It Works

Tap the microphone and describe your event in plain language. VoxPlan sends your audio to a secure transcription service, extracts event details like the title, date, time, and location, and creates the event in your chosen calendar. Audio and transcripts are not stored after processing.

Voice understanding is powered by Mistral AI, Europe’s leading AI lab, with secure processing designed to support GDPR-compliant handling of your data.

Calendar Integrations

VoxPlan integrates with the calendars you already use:

  • iOS Calendar — Access your on-device calendars through Apple's EventKit framework

Calendar data is used only to display and manage your events. It is never sold, shared for advertising, or used for tracking.
